About AI House


Are you passionate about artificial intelligence? Do you want to be part of a community of like-minded individuals who are working on cutting-edge projects? If so, AI House LA is for you.

The AI house is a place where people who are passionate about AI can come together to live, work, and play. We have all the amenities you need to be productive, including high-speed internet, a comfortable workspace, and a kitchen that is stocked with all the food and drinks you need. But we also know that work isn't everything, so we'll a games room, a movie room, and a rooftop deck where you can relax and have fun.

So if you're looking for a place to call home while you're working on your AI projects, look no further than the hacker house!
